In 2017 we saw a startling number of apparel retailers closing their doors or filing for bankruptcy. The internet, and most notably smartphones, have changed consumer demand and purchasing patterns. With people now spending more than 5 hours every day on their phones, retailers can no longer expect to attract their customers' dollars if they are not equipped to compete in the new mobile economy. Otherwise they'll quickly join the retailers shuttering their doors. To be a leader in this new mobile economy requires merging your offline success with a matching online experience to strengthen customer relationships and cultivate brand loyalty. The better omnichannel experience you can provide for your customers, the more you stand out as the superior solution in your market. We have the tools and the team to take you there, and we'll help you lead the way in this new, mobile era of retail.

Brick & mortar stores are a great foundation for building your brand and customer experience. They propel loyalty through your website and mobile app.

Consumer shopping behavior has changed to facilitate shopping when, where, and however is most convenient. US ecommerce sales will surpass $400bn in 2018.

App usage takes up 90% of all time spent on mobile devices. In 2017, for the first time ever, mobile ecommerce surpassed desktop during the holiday shopping season.
